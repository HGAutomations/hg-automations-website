# Content Rules

- Do not invent testimonials
- Do not invent client logos
- Do not invent case studies
- Do not invent metrics, percentages, cost savings, or response rates
- Do not claim certifications or audits unless the user explicitly confirmed them
- DSGVO conformity may be stated because the user explicitly confirmed it
- Focus on clear business value: Erreichbarkeit, Terminstruktur, Entlastung, Priorisierung, Prozessklarheit
- Explain the AI voice bot in practical business language
- Keep the website understandable for non-technical business owners
- Mention the chatbot and dashboard carefully if they are not fully live yet
- Avoid infrastructure-heavy language about webhooks, APIs, or vendors on public-facing sections unless needed
